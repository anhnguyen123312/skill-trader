## 1. CANG THANG DIA CHINH TRI VA CHIEN TRANH

### Co che tac dong
- **Tam ly so hai**: Nha dau tu rut von khoi tai san rui ro, chuyen sang vang
- **Gian doan chuoi cung ung**: Day gia nang luong/luong thuc, tao lam phat
- **NHTW mua vang**: Cac quoc gia bi trung phat tang cuong mua vang
- **Suy yeu USD**: Chi phi chien tranh lam tang tham hut ngan sach

## 2. CHIEN TRANH THUONG MAI VA THUE QUAN

### Co che tac dong
- Bat on dinh kinh te toan cau -> tim den vang
- Lam phat chi phi day (thue quan tang gia nhap khau)
- Suy yeu USD do tham hut thuong mai
- NHTW buoc cat lai suat

**Tuong quan:** Moi 1% tang doanh thu thue quan My = 0.9% tang gia vang (Goldman Sachs)

### Vi du: Trump 2.0 (2025)

| Thoi diem | Su kien | Gia vang | Bien dong |
|-----------|---------|----------|-----------|
| Q1/2025 | Thue quan 145% hang TQ | $3,200+ | Pha $3,000 |
| 04/2025 | Leo thang toan dien | $3,896 | Ky luc moi |
| 10/2025 | Thue quan 100%, TQ han che dat hiem | $4,058-$4,381 | Dinh lich su |

### Chi bao dan dat
- Phat ngon lanh dao My-Trung ve thuong mai
- Bien dong ti gia CNY/USD
- PMI xuat khau Trung Quoc
- Chi so gia nhap khau My

## 3. SU KIEN THIEN NGA DEN (BLACK SWAN)

### Co che (2 giai doan)
- **Giai doan 1 (0-2 tuan)**: BAN THAO do thanh ly cuong buc, giam 5-15%
- **Giai doan 2 (2 tuan - 2 nam)**: NHTW bom thanh khoan -> lam phat ky vong tang -> vang tang 50-150%

### Vi du lich su

| Su kien | Giam ban dau | Tang sau do |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| COVID-19 (2020) | -4.5% (03/2020) | +42% (den 08/2020: $2,067) |
| GFC 2008 | -28 den -33% | +166% (den 09/2011: $1,895) |

**Bai hoc:** Nha dau tu kinh nghiem MUA vang khi gia giam trong khung hoang thanh khoan.

## 5. TIN HIEU THI TRUONG TRAI PHIEU

### Co che
- **Lai suat thuc am**: Giu trai phieu bi "phat" -> vang hap dan hon
- **Duong cong dao nguoc**: Tin hieu suy thoai -> Fed cat lai suat -> vang tang
- **Spread tin dung mo rong**: Rui ro pha san tang -> chuyen sang vang

### Dac biet 2024-2025
Vang pha vo tuong quan truyen thong voi lai suat. Mac du lai suat thuc van duong (~2%), vang van tang manh nho NHTW mua vang va rui ro dia chinh tri -- **su thay doi co cau (structural shift)**.

### Chi bao dan dat
- TIPS Breakeven Inflation > 3%
- Spread 2Y-10Y chuyen tu dao nguoc sang duong (steepening)
- High-Yield Spread > 500bps
- 10Y Treasury giam nhanh duoi 3.5%

## 6. PHI USD HOA (DE-DOLLARIZATION)

### Co che
- NHTW da dang hoa du tru: Giam USD, tang vang
- He thong thanh toan thay the: BRICS Pay, CIPS giam phu thuoc SWIFT
- Du an "Unit" (10/2025): Dong tien BRICS bao dam 40% vang
- Mat niem tin: Dong bang du tru Nga ($300 ty) nam 2022 la cot moc lich su
