## 1. LAI SUAT VA CHINH SACH TIEN TE

### 1.1. Co che tac dong

Vang la tai san khong sinh loi (non-yielding asset). Lai suat anh huong den vang thong qua **chi phi co hoi** (opportunity cost):

- **Khi lai suat tang**: Trai phieu va tien gui tro nen hap dan hon. Nha dau tu chuyen von tu vang sang cac tai san sinh loi, tao ap luc giam gia vang.
- **Khi lai suat giam**: Chi phi co hoi nam giu vang giam. Dong tien chay vao vang nhu kenh bao toan gia tri.

Yeu to then chot nhat la **lai suat thuc** (real yields = lai suat danh nghia - lam phat ky vong), do bang loi suat trai phieu TIPS 10 nam.

## 3. LAM PHAT VA DU LIEU CPI/PCE

### 3.1. Co che tac dong

Vang la **hang rao chong lam phat**. Lam phat cao lam mat gia tri tien te phap dinh; vang giu duoc gia tri. Tac dong gian tiep qua lai suat thuc: lam phat cao + Fed khong tang du nhanh = lai suat thuc giam = vang tang.

### 3.2. Huong tuong quan

**THUAN CHIEU** voi lam phat cao (nhung phuc tap). Vang cho loi nhuan thuc duong trong **67%** cac giai doan lam phat cao (>3%) ke tu 1971.

### 3.3. Vi du lich su

| Nam | CPI cuoi nam (YoY) | PCE binh quan nam | Thay doi gia vang |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2024 | 2.9% | 2.6% | +26% |
| 2025 | 2.7% | ~2.8% (core cuoi nam: 3.0%) | +65% |

- **Thap nien 1970 (Stagflation)**: Lam phat TB 7.1%/nam, vang tang **+2,329%**
- **2024-2025**: Lam phat duoi 3% nhung vang van tang 65% -- vai tro mo rong thanh "tai san tru an tong hop"

### 3.4. Du lieu can theo doi

- CPI (Bureau of Labor Statistics): Hang thang, ngay 10-15
- Core CPI (loai tru thuc pham va nang luong)
- PCE Price Index: Cuoi thang, chi so uu thich cua Fed
- Core PCE: Muc tieu 2% cua Fed
- Breakeven Inflation Rate (5Y, 10Y)
- Michigan Consumer Sentiment -- Inflation Expectations

### 3.5. Muc do phan ung gia

- CPI cao hon ky vong 0.1-0.2%: Tang $10-30/oz
- PCE releases: $20-60/oz
- Cu soc lam phat lon (>0.5% so voi ky vong): $50-100/oz
