## 1. LAI SUAT VA CHINH SACH TIEN TE

### Co che
- Vang = tai san khong sinh loi -> lai suat tang = chi phi co hoi tang = vang giam
- Yeu to then chot: **Lai suat thuc** (real yields = nominal - inflation)
- Tuong quan: **-0.82** (nghich manh)
- Moi 1% lai suat thuc giam -> vang tang ~$400/oz

### Chu ky cat giam lai suat Fed 2024-2025

| Giai doan | Lai suat | Tac dong vang |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| Dau 2024 | 5.25%-5.50% | Vang ~$2,000-$2,100 |
| Nua cuoi 2024 | 4.25%-4.50% (-100bps) | Vang $2,600+ |
| T9/2025 | 4.00%-4.25% (-25bps) | Vang $3,600+ |
| T10/2025 | 3.75%-4.00% (-25bps) | Vang $4,000 |
| T12/2025 | 3.50%-3.75% (-25bps) | Vang $4,300+ |

### Muc phan ung gia
- Cat 25bps (duoc ky vong): $20-50/oz
- Cat bat ngo/manh hon: $50-150/oz
- Hawkish bat ngo: Giam $30-80/oz
- Dot Plot thay doi lon: $50-100/oz

### Data can theo doi
- Cuoc hop FOMC (8 lan/nam)
- Bien ban FOMC (3 tuan sau hop)
- Dot Plot (hang quy: T3, 6, 9, 12)
- Loi suat TIPS 10Y (FRED)
- CME FedWatch Tool

## 3. LAM PHAT (CPI/PCE)

### Co che
- Vang = hang rao chong lam phat (inflation hedge)
- Lam phat cao -> dong tien mat gia -> vang giu gia tri
- Ky vong lam phat tang -> vang tang truoc

### Tuong quan: **Thuan chieu** voi lam phat cao, nhung phuc tap
- Vang loi nhuan thuc duong trong **67%** cac giai doan lam phat >3%

### Du lieu CPI/PCE vs Vang

| Nam | CPI | PCE | Vang thay doi |
|-----|-----|-----|---------------|
| 2024 | 2.9% | 2.6% | +26% |
| 2025 | 2.7% | ~2.8% (core 3.0%) | +65% |

### Vi du lich su
- **1970s (Stagflation)**: Lam phat TB 7.1%/nam -> Vang **+2,329%**
- **2020-2024**: Lam phat TB 4.1% -> Vang +45%
- **2024-2025**: Lam phat <3% nhung vang +65% -> vai tro mo rong sang "safe haven tong hop"

### Muc phan ung
- CPI cao hon ky vong 0.1-0.2%: Vang +$10-30/oz
- PCE releases: $20-60/oz (quan trong hon CPI)
- Cu soc lam phat >0.5%: Vang $50-100/oz

### Data can theo doi
- CPI (hang thang, ngay 10-15)
- Core CPI (quan trong hon tong the)
- PCE Price Index (cuoi thang - thuo do uu thich cua Fed)
- Core PCE (muc tieu Fed = 2%)
- Breakeven Inflation Rate 5Y/10Y (FRED)

## 4. GDP VA TANG TRUONG KINH TE

### Co che
- Kinh te manh -> dau tu rui ro tang -> vang giam (risk-on)
- Kinh te yeu -> safe haven tang -> vang tang (risk-off)
- GDP yeu -> Fed noi long -> vang tang (gian tiep)

### Tuong quan: **Nghich chieu** (phi tuyen)

### GDP My vs Vang

| Nam | GDP | Vang TB | Nhan xet |
|-----|-----|---------|----------|
| 2024 | 2.8% | $2,387 | Kinh te vung nhung vang van tang |
| 2025 | 2.2% (Q4: 1.4%) | $3,431 | Tang truong cham, vang +44% |

### Hieu suat vang trong suy thoai

| Suy thoai | Vang | S&P 500 |
|-----------|------|---------|
| 1973-1975 | +35%/nam | Giam manh |
| 2007-2009 | Tang manh | -57% tu dinh |
| 2020 | Tang manh | Giam roi hoi phuc |

Vang vuot troi S&P 500 trong **6/8 cuoc suy thoai** tu 1971, TB **+37%**

### Muc phan ung
- GDP thap hon ky vong 0.5-1.0%: Vang +$20-50/oz
- GDP am bat ngo: Vang +$50-150/oz
- NFP yeu >50,000 viec: Vang +$20-60/oz
- ISM PMI <50: Vang +$15-40/oz

### Data can theo doi
